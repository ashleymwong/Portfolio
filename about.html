About Me

Mechanical Integrated Engineering Student at UBC

I'm a Mechanical Integrated Engineering Student at the University of British Columbia with a passion for mechatronic engineering. I'm interested in innovative and sustainable technology, and am always eager for a challenge. I'm active in leadership and mentorship among my community and strive to improve people's quality of life.

Education

Bachelor of Applied Science in Mechanical Engineering — University of British Columbia, Vancouver, BC. My coursework spans mechanical design, control systems, thermodynamics, finite element analysis, and mechatronics, giving me a strong foundation across both hardware and software disciplines.

What Drives Me

I'm drawn to problems that sit at the intersection of mechanical design and intelligent systems — whether that's building a robotic hand, designing subsystems for a human-powered submarine, or developing wearable assistive technology. I believe good engineering should be both technically rigorous and deeply human-centered.

Beyond Engineering

Outside of engineering, I'm actively involved in mentorship and community leadership. I believe in lifting others as I grow, and I take pride in creating spaces where people feel supported and challenged to do their best work.

Technical Skills

My toolkit includes SolidWorks, Fusion 360, GD&T, CNC machining, 3D printing, rapid prototyping, FEA, MATLAB, Python, and Arduino. I'm comfortable taking a project from concept sketches through to physical prototype and analysis.

  • CAD & Design — SolidWorks, Fusion 360, GD&T, Technical Drawings
  • Manufacturing — CNC Machining, 3D Printing, DFM/DFA, Rapid Prototyping
  • Analysis — FEA, Tolerance Analysis, Root Cause Analysis, Testing & Validation
  • Programming — MATLAB, Python, Arduino, Automation
